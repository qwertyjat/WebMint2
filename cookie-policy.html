Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we use

This website uses your browser's local storage — not traditional tracking cookies — to remember one thing: whether you've chosen light or dark mode. This value never leaves your browser.

2. What we don't use

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or cross-site tracking on this website by default.

3. Managing local storage

You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this website, or by using a private/incognito window.

4. If analytics are added later

Should we add an analytics tool in the future, this policy will be updated to disclose exactly what's collected and why.
