Process

From first message to live site.

A straightforward, five-step process — the same one for every project, so you always know what's next.

01

Discovery call

We talk through your business, your customers, and what the website needs to achieve — usually a 15–20 minute call or chat.

02

Proposal & plan

You get a clear scope, timeline, and price in writing before any work begins — no surprises later.

03

Design

We design the key pages first so you can see and approve the direction before the full site is built.

04

Build & review

The full site is built, tested on real devices, and sent to you for a review round before launch.

05

Launch & support

We deploy your site, connect your domain, and stay on for 30 days of free support after you go live.
