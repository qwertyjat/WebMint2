About us

A small studio, built for small businesses.

We started WebMint because too many good local businesses were being represented online by outdated, slow, or copy-pasted websites. We build the alternative — fast, considered, and easy to keep updated.

Why WebMint exists

Most small businesses either overpay a big agency for a bloated website, or end up with a free template that looks like a hundred others. We sit in between: agency-quality design and code, at a price and timeline that makes sense for a local business.

Every project starts with a conversation about how your business actually works — not a generic questionnaire — and ends with a site you can genuinely keep updated yourself.
